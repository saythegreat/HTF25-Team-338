World War II Essay

History 201 • Professor Dr. Williams

Due Dec 22

Due Dec 22, 2024

Essay Instructions

Write a comprehensive 1500-word essay analyzing the causes and consequences of World War II.

Essay Requirements:

  • Length: 1500 words minimum
  • Format: MLA style with proper citations
  • Sources: Minimum 5 academic sources
  • Structure: Introduction, body paragraphs, conclusion
  • Focus: Causes leading to war and long-term consequences

Submission: Submit as Word document (.docx) through the course portal.

Suggested Essay Outline

I. Introduction

Thesis statement and overview of WWII's impact

II. Causes of WWII

Treaty of Versailles, economic factors, rise of fascism

III. Major Events

Key battles, turning points, major campaigns

IV. Consequences

Political, economic, and social impacts

V. Conclusion

Summary and lasting significance
